About the CVR College of Engineering Alumni Portal

The CVR College of Engineering Alumni Portal is a platform designed for alumni of the college to connect with each other, share their experiences and memories, and stay informed about the latest news and events at the college. Through this portal, alumni can access exclusive content, participate in alumni events, and stay in touch with their alma mater.
